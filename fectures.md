### Public Pages

### Authentication
- **Login (`/login`)**: User sign-in page.
- **Register (`/register`)**: New user sign-up page.
- **Verify (`/verify`)**: Email or account verification page.

### Student/User Pages
- **Dashboard/Profile (`/profile`)**: Personal user dashboard and profile management.
- **My Enrollments (`/enrollments`)**: Access to all purchased and enrolled courses.
- **Courses Catalog (`/courses`)**: Browse and search for available courses.
- **Course Details (`/courses/[id]`)**: Detailed view of a specific course.
- **Checkout (`/checkout`)**: Secure payment and course purchase page.
- **Wishlist (`/wishlist`)**: Saved courses for future purchase.
- **My Learning Paths (`/learning-paths`)**: Curated paths for specific skills.
- **Certificates (`/certificate`)**: View and download earned certificates.
- **Messages (`/messages`)**: Real-time chat with friends and AI assistant.
- **Network (`/network`)**: Manage friend requests and social connections.
- **Public Profile (`/user/[id]` or `/@username`)**: Publicly visible user profile.
- **Settings (`/settings`)**: Account, security, and notification settings.
- **Subscription (`/subscription`)**: Manage active subscriptions.
- **Referrals (`/referrals`)**: Referral program dashboard.

### Community & Social
- **Community Feed (`/community`)**: Social feed with posts from other users.
- **Hashtag Feed (`/hashtag/[tag]`)**: View posts related to specific topics.

### Admin Pages (`/admin`)
- **Admin Dashboard**: Overview of platform analytics and stats.
- **Course Management**: Create and edit courses, lectures, and curriculum.
- **User Management**: Manage student and instructor accounts.
- **Content Management**: Manage blogs, careers, and mentor listings.
- **Gamification**: Create and manage badges and achievement rules.
- **Testimonial Moderation**: Review and approve user testimonials.
- **Support Desk**: Manage support tickets and customer queries.
- **Audit Logs**: View logs of administrative actions.
- **Analytics**: Detailed reports on sales, users, and engagement.

## ‚ú® Platform Features

### Learning Management
- **Video Lectures**: High-quality video streaming for course content.
- **Course Progress Tracking**: Real-time tracking of lecture completion.
- **Assessments & Quizzes**: Interactive quizzes to test knowledge.
- **Assignments**: Submit and track course assignments.
- **Lecture Versions**: Manage different versions of lecture content.
- **Live Classes**: Integration for real-time live teaching sessions.
- **Notes**: Take and save personal notes during lectures.

### AI-Powered Features (Gemini)
- **AI Chat Assistant**: 24/7 support for answering course-related questions.
- **Smart Summaries**: Automatically generated summaries for lectures.
- **Interactive AI Quizzes**: AI-generated quizzes based on course material.
- **Transcript Generation**: Automated transcripts for video content.

### Social & Community
- **Friend System**: Send, accept, and manage friend requests.
- **Real-time Messaging**: Instant chat between users.
- **Community Posts**: Create text/image posts, like, and comment.
- **Reposting**: Share other users' content to your feed.
- **Hashtags**: Categorize and discover content using hashtags.

### Gamification & Rewards
- **Badges**: Earn digital badges for milestones and completions.
- **Streaks**: Daily login and learning streaks to encourage consistency.
- **Certificates**: Automated PDF certificate generation with QR code verification.

### Professional & Career
- **Mentor Matching**: Connect with industry experts for guidance.
- **Career Listings**: Browse and apply for job opportunities.
- **Public Portfolio**: Showcase certificates and achievements on a public profile.

### Administrative & Security
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Different permissions for Students, Mentors, and Admins.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Enhanced account security.
- **Audit Trails**: Immutable logs for all sensitive admin operations.
- **Support Ticket System**: Structured support with queues and SLA tracking.
- **Drive Upload Integration**: Support for uploading large files to Google Drive.

### Commerce
- **Razorpay Integration**: Secure payment processing for courses and subscriptions.
- **Coupon System**: Create and manage discount codes.
- **Subscription Models**: Support for recurring billing and tiered access.
